Research Areas

  • A little history

    • 2000 Mauro Pezzè founds LTA Laboratory of Testing and Analysis at DISCo (Dipartimento di Informatics Sistemistica e Comunicazione) of Università degli studi di Milano Bicocca in Milan. The lab is a twin institution with many common projects and interests.
    • 2006 Mauro Pezzè founds STAR Software Testing and Analysis Research Team at the Faculty of informatics of USI Università della Svizzera Italiana in Lugano.
    • 2020 Mauro Pezzè founds STAR Software Testing and Analysis Research Team at the Schaffhausen Institute of Technology in Schaffhausen

  • Background and expertise

    we are leaders in:
    • software testing
      1. testing concurrent systems
      2. testing interactive applications
      3. test case generation
      4. test evolution and reuse
      5. oracle generation and improvement
    • program analysis
      1. symbolic execution
      2. dynamic analysis
    • self-healing systems
      1. automatic workaround
      2. failure prediction and fault localization
